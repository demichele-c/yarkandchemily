Saturday Schedule

7:30 AM - Chuck and Emily leave Philadelphia

🚗 Estimated travel time to Easton: 1.5 hours

☕ Grab some coffee and breakfast to go before leaving.

📸 Content Opportunity: Capture the morning vibe with a quick selfie while grabbing coffee—start the day with smiles!

9:00 AM - Pick up Mark and Yamila in Easton, PA

📍 Easton, PA on Apple Maps

🚙 Aim to leave Easton by 9:15 AM to stay on schedule.

📸 Content Opportunity: A candid shot of the couples reuniting and getting excited about the day ahead.

10:15 AM - Arrive at 288 N Stagecoach Rd for Whitewater Rafting

📍 Whitewater Challengers Poconos on Apple Maps

🚣 Check-in with Whitewater Challengers Poconos for the rafting adventure.

🎒 Ensure everyone has proper attire (swimsuits, water shoes, sunscreen).

💧 Remember to bring a change of clothes and towels for after the adventure.

🌐 Whitewater Challengers Website

📸 Content Opportunity: Capture everyone in their rafting gear before hitting the water—a perfect mix of excitement and style.

11:00 AM - 2:00 PM - Whitewater Rafting Adventure

Enjoy the 3-hour rafting experience through the scenic Poconos.

Detail: The rafting trip will take you through class I-II rapids, ideal for a fun and safe adventure. Expect beautiful surroundings with forested riverbanks and the rush of the Lehigh River.

📸 Content Opportunity: Mid-rafting action shots (if you have a waterproof camera) or photos from designated calm areas.

2:15 PM - Finish rafting and head back to the car

👕 Change into dry clothes.

🗺 Drive to Hickory Run State Park (approximately 20-minute drive).

📸 Content Opportunity: Group photo at the end of the rafting trip, holding paddles high—celebrating a thrilling experience!

2:45 PM - Arrive at Hickory Run State Park & Set Up Camp

📍 Hickory Run State Park on Apple Maps

⛺ Find your reserved campsite and set up tents and campfire.

🛋 Relax and unwind after the rafting trip.

🥤 Snack time: Enjoy some light snacks from the cooler (fruit, chips, etc.).

📸 Content Opportunity: Document setting up camp—smiles all around and a relaxed vibe as you settle into nature.

5:00 PM - Prepare and Cook Dinner

🍔 Grill burgers over the campfire.

🥗 Bring side dishes (salad, chips, veggies) and beverages (soda, water, maybe wine for the girls).

🔥 Enjoy dinner together and share stories around the campfire.

🍫 Don’t forget marshmallows, chocolate, and graham crackers for s’mores!

📸 Content Opportunity: Capture the warm glow of the campfire and happy faces enjoying a delicious meal—highlighting the coziness of camping.

7:00 PM - Campfire Time

🃏 Play card games designed for couples to spark fun conversations and laughter.

🎲 Enjoy other couple activities such as charades or storytelling.

❤️ Consider some activities like a "How Well Do You Know Your Partner" quiz.

📸 Content Opportunity: Couple’s selfie by the campfire, with the night sky filled with stars above—creating a romantic ambiance.

10:00 PM - Prepare for Bed

🌜 Wind down and head to bed in your tents.

📸 Content Opportunity: Capture the peaceful night with tents silhouetted against the moonlight—ending the day on a serene note.

Sunday Schedule

8:30 AM - Wake Up and Make Breakfast

🥪 Enjoy Mark's famous breakfast sandwiches to start the day.

☕ Serve with coffee, tea, and juice.

📸 Content Opportunity: Morning light filtering through the trees as everyone enjoys breakfast—showcasing a perfect morning in the great outdoors.

10:00 AM - Pack Up Camp

🎒 Clean up the campsite, pack tents, and gather all belongings.

11:00 AM - Start the Hike on Shades of Death Trail

🚶 Directions: From the camping area, head towards the trailhead of Shades of Death Trail.

🛤 Trail length: About 2.2 miles, moderate difficulty.

Detail: This trail offers a stunning walk through a hemlock and rhododendron forest, complete with scenic waterfalls, streams, and lush greenery. It’s a perfect spot for capturing natural beauty.

📸 Content Opportunity: Take photos near waterfalls and along the streams—ideal for romantic nature shots.

1:00 PM - Finish Hike and Head to Lunch

Drive to a local spot near the park for lunch.

3:00 PM - Return Home

🏠 Head back to Easton to say goodbye.

🛋 Rest and relax after an exciting weekend.

📸 Content Opportunity: Final farewell photos—everyone heading home with great memories and happy hearts.
